Yoga is a physical, mental, and spiritual practice that originated in ancient India. The word "yoga" comes from the Sanskrit word "yuj" which means "to unite" or "to join." Yoga involves a variety of practices, including postures and poses, breathing exercises, meditation, and relaxation techniques. The ultimate goal of yoga is to achieve a state of inner peace, self-awareness, and union with the divine. Yoga has many different styles and traditions, and it is practiced by people all over the world for a wide range of reasons, including fitness, stress relief, and spiritual growth.
